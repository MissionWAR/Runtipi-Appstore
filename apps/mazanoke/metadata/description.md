Mazanoke is a self-hosted local image optimizer that runs entirely in your browser, so your photos never leave your device.

Compress and convert images instantly with adjustable quality, target file size and max dimensions while EXIF data is automatically stripped for better privacy.

It supports common formats like JPG, PNG and WebP, can convert from HEIC, AVIF, GIF, SVG, TIFF and more, and works as an installable PWA that stays fast, simple and offline-friendly for everyday use with family and friends.
